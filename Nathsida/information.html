Natalie Karlsson, A.k.a. LittleLionLie

Natalie Karlsson (LittleLionLie) is an artist who lives in Sweden. She loves drawing and is testing new artstyles all the time. Shedraws almost anything; everything from adorable-super-cutsie stuff to morbid horror.

Aside from her art she has interests in stuff like youtube, games, cartoons and music. She really likes to draw fanart of de various youtubers and characters, which you also can see on this website.

She's currently studying comics in high school and loves it.

She's available on various sites, e.g. Instagram and Deviantart, which you can find under links.

I hope you'll find her art just as cool and cute as me.
